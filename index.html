Great Lantern Festival

Sat Sep 26, 2026 (4–9 PM)

Derr-Okamoto Park, Elk Grove, California · free admission · lantern procession at dusk

We're bringing Great Lantern Festival back to Elk Grove. Rooted in Tết Trung Thu — the Vietnamese Mid-Autumn celebration — it is a cherished night when families gather under the full moon: children carry lanterns, share mooncakes, and drink tea. This year's celebration is hosted by the Vietnamese American community through VACOS, cPALSs, and NVCC.
